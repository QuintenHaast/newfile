Top Tapas Locations

Perikete

Carrer de Llauder, 6, Barcelona

Ciutat Vella

Quality: ★★★★☆

Price: €€

Atmosphere: ★★☆☆☆

With entire pig legs hanging from the ceiling, Perikete is a very cozy and authentic tapas bar. The typical Spanish tapas and drinks are very affordable. Reservations can only be made by phone and can be quite difficult, but this is not necessary as long as you are not with a very large group. After a maximum of 10-20 minutes of waiting across the street, you will be quickly guided to your table.
