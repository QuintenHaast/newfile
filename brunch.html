Top Brunch Locations

PicNic

C/ del Comerç, 1, Barcelona

Ciutat Vella

Quality: ★★★★☆

Price: €€

Atmosphere: ★★☆☆☆

Just out of sight of the Arc de Triomf, this place's location does not do it justice. Picnic offers a wide selection of original and relatively modern breakfast, lunch, and snack dishes. A definite recommendation is the burrata salad or the carrot cake. The service is also very friendly!

Eixample

Quality: ★★★★☆

Brunells

Carrer de la Princesa, 22, Barcelona

Ciutat Vella

Quality: ★★★★☆

Price: €€

Atmosphere: ★★☆☆☆

This bakery is now very well-known on social media for its trendy filled croissants. The bakery offers all kinds of delicious pastries, but the pistachio croissant is an absolute must-try.
